Organization Lore

Founded in 2120 amidst the chaos of the Third Corporate War, Shadow Corporation emerged as a response to the unchecked power of global megacorporations. What began as a collective of elite hackers, military specialists, and corporate defectors has evolved into one of the most sophisticated covert operations networks in the known sectors.

Our mission extends beyond the conventional boundaries of corporate and military operations. We specialize in high-risk, high-reward strategic initiatives that conventional organizations cannot—or will not—undertake.

Shadow Corporation operatives are known for their unparalleled expertise, adaptability, and absolute loyalty to the organization's objectives. Our decentralized command structure and compartmentalized operations ensure that we remain several steps ahead of our adversaries.
